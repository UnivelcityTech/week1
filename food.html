Carbohydrates

Carbohydrate is one of the three micronutrients through which a human body acquires energy. It consists of carbon, hydrogen and oxygen atoms at its chemical level.

  • glucose
  • starch
  • lactose
  • soft drinks
  • popcorn
Fat and Oil

Fats are solid at room temperature whereas oils are liquids. Hibernating animals survive by using up their stores of body fat. Some plant seeds survive because they contain a store of oil. Fats and oils do not mix with water.

  • Butter
  • coconut oil
  • palm oil
  • goose fat
  • hard margarines
